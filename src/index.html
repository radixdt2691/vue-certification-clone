Benefits of getting your Vue.js Certification

Demonstrate your Vue.js competency

Earning a Vue.js certification showcases that you have the knowledge and skills required to build complex applications using Vue.js. This level of expertise can be attractive to potential employers or clients, as it demonstrates your commitment to staying up-to-date with the latest technologies and your ability to deliver high-quality, professional-level work.

Enhanced Career Opportunities

Vue.js is a popular front-end framework, and many companies are looking for skilled Vue.js developers. Earning a Vue.js certification can help set you apart from other candidates and make you a more attractive candidate for job openings. Additionally, a certification can lead to higher pay and better career advancement opportunities, as you'll have the skills and qualifications necessary to take on more challenging roles and responsibilities.

How Certification can benefit your team

Becoming a Certified Vue.js developer can benefit your team with improved morale, productivity, skills, and efficiency. Offering this certification motivates developers to learn new skills, build and ship better products faster, and broaden the variety of skills available. Your team can also improve competence and confidence to handle any task, leading to better knowledge retention and efficiency.

We are releasing the Certification program progressively, with a number of releases planned over the course of the next few months. The Certified Vue.js Developer examination question sets and code challenges are reviewed by the Vue.js Core Team, including Evan You, and we will only release the examinations when all parties are satisfied with the standard of the exam and after a thorough review and testing process.

Here is the current release schedule:

Any customers who pre-order now will have early access to the Certified Vue.js Developer Preparation Guide, which will give you an overview of what will be tested in the related exam. All pre-order customers will also have access to the Certification Discord server, so you can interact with other developers preparing for the exam, along with the Certification team.

If you pre-order either the Exam & Preparation bundle or the Exam, Preparation & Boot Camp bundle, you will also be able to have early access to the Self-Study training and begin preparing for the exams with code challenges and quizzes. The Self Study training module is ready to get started with studying and solving code challenges with the first chapters. More content is currently under development and will be released constantly until it is 100% complete.

We are expecting that the Vue.js Developer Certification Exam will be launched in June 2023 following the review and sign-off process of the Vue team. In this same period, we will also introduce the Online Boot Camp live workshops related to the level 1 exam.

In Q3 of this year, we aim to start releasing the Senior Vue.js Developer Certification products, including the related Preparation Guide with the full curriculum, the self-training preparation material, and the Boot Camp live workshops. The Senior Vue.js Developer examination will also only be made available once the same rigorous testing and review process has been completed.

Any product updates will be regularly communicated via email and Discord announcements, so make sure to keep checking your inbox.
